Who We Are?

We supply wide range of medical devices and healthcare products to all government hospitals and private clinics throughout the country .

With the ambition of establishing direct and lasting relationships with our customers, QAREMEDIC Sdn Bhd (QMSB) began trading in October 2012 . QMSB are certified Good Distribution Practice For Medical Devices (GDPMD) company in Malaysia. QMSB ensures at all time the quality, safety and performance of medical devices and healthcare products in the supply chains are well maintained.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of exceptional medical products and services globally, known for delivering unmatched value and building enduring relationships with our customers to advance healthcare and improve lives.

Our Mission

To grow our business by providing quality products and services at the great value whenever and wherever by building positive and lasting relationships with our customers.
