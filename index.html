WHO WE ARE

We supply a wide range of medical devices and healthcare products to all government hospitals and private clinics throughout the country.

With the ambition of establishing direct and lasting relationships with our customers, QAREMEDIC Sdn Bhd (QMSB) began trading in October 2012. QMSB is a certified Good Distribution Practice For Medical Devices (GDPMD) company in Malaysia. QMSB ensures at all times that the quality, safety, and performance of medical devices and healthcare products in the supply chains are well maintained.
